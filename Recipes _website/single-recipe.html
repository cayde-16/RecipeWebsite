Instructions

Preparation and Mixing:

Start by gathering your ingredients: ripe bananas, all-purpose flour, baking powder, salt, eggs, milk (or a dairy-free alternative), sugar (optional), and a touch of vanilla extract for extra flavor. In a mixing bowl, mash the ripe bananas until smooth using a fork or potato masher. Add the flour, baking powder, and a pinch of salt to the mashed bananas. Crack in the eggs, pour in the milk, and add a splash of vanilla extract. If desired, add a bit of sugar to enhance sweetness. Gently stir the ingredients until just combined. Don't overmix; a few lumps are okay for fluffy pancakes.

Cooking:

Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat. You can lightly grease it with butter or cooking spray. Once the skillet is hot, ladle a portion of the pancake batter onto the cooking surface. Use the back of the ladle to spread the batter into a round shape. Cook the pancakes for about 2-3 minutes on the first side, or until you see bubbles forming on the surface. Carefully flip the pancakes using a spatula and cook for another 1-2 minutes on the second side, or until both sides are golden brown and cooked through.

Serving:

Once cooked, transfer the banana pancakes to a serving plate. Garnish the pancakes with slices of fresh banana for an extra burst of flavor and visual appeal. Optionally, sprinkle chopped nuts (such as walnuts or pecans) over the pancakes for added crunch. Drizzle with maple syrup, honey, or your preferred sweet topping. Serve the banana pancakes warm and enjoy the delightful blend of flavors and textures!
